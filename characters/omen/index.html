Personality

Omen is quiet, watchful, and impossibly ancient in demeanor. Though often perceived as frightening, they possess a strange melancholy kindness, appearing most often beside those standing at the edge of great change or ruin. To meet their gaze is said to feel like remembering something humanity was never meant to know.

Magic

Omen's abilities defy mortal categorization, existing somewhere between prophecy, divinity, and cosmic ruin. Their presence distorts the night sky itself, and ancient records speak of stars dimming in their wake. Whether these phenomena are intentional acts or mere consequences of their existence remains unknown.
The Starscorched Omen does not cast spells in any conventional sense. Reality simply bends around them. Dreams sour, compasses fail, and the heavens shift subtly beneath their gaze.
They carry fragments of a dying cosmos within their soul. From their mane drift ashes of collapsed stars, and from their voice comes the hollow quiet between constellations.

Relationships

Omen is not concerned with mortal relationships, but they are often found in the company of those on the brink of great change or catastrophe. They are drawn to individuals who stand at the edge of transformation, whether it be a hero facing a final battle, a ruler on the verge of downfall, or a world teetering on the edge of collapse. Omen's presence is both a harbinger and a witness to these pivotal moments in history.
